About Our Journal

World Journal of Biotechnology and Bioengineering (WJBB) is an Open access, Peer-reviewed, monthly Journal, publishes all the basic and applied research and technologies in the field of Biotechnology & Bioengineering.

Aims and Scope

World Journal of Biotechnology and Bioengineering (WJBB) is an international wide scope, peer-reviewed open access journal for the publication of original papers concerned with diverse aspects of bio-science application, technology and engineering.

WJBB aims to present full-length original research articles and reviews, short communications, case studies and letters to editor in all areas of biotechnological and bioengineering research. It emphasizes understanding and advancing of the basics and applied biotechnology and bioengineering including any related economic, environmental and policy issues for the betterment of mankind.

Areas and subareas of interest including but not limited to

  • Animal Biotechnology
  • Medical Biotechnology
  • Plant Biotechnology
  • Microbial Biotechnology
  • Marine biotechnology
  • Environmental biotechnology
  • Biotechnology for renewable energy (Bioenergy including Biofuel)
  • Biochemistry of the living cell
  • Inorganic biochemistry
  • Protein chemistry
  • Enzymology
  • Cytology
  • Neurobiology
  • Enzyme systems and their applications, including enzyme reactors, purification, and applied aspects of protein engineering
  • Animal-cell biotechnology, including media development
  • Applied aspects of cellular physiology, metabolism, and energetics
  • Biocatalysis and applied enzymology, including enzyme reactors, protein engineering, and nanobiotechnology
  • Biothermodynamics
  • Biomaterials, including delivery systems and materials for tissue engineering
  • Bioprocess engineering, including kinetics and modeling of biological systems, transport phenomena in bioreactors, bioreactor design, monitoring, and control
  • Biosensors and instrumentation
  • Computational and systems biology, including bioinformatics and genomic/proteomic studies
  • Environmental biotechnology, including biofilms, algal systems, and bioremediation
  • Metabolic and cellular engineering
  • Spectroscopic, Chromatographic and other analytical techniques for biotechnological applications
  • Synthetic biology
  • Tissue engineering, stem-cell bioengineering, regenerative medicine, gene therapy and delivery systems
  • Biological processes and industrial applications
  • Bioinformatics and Bioenergetics
  • Innovation in biotechnology and bioethics and more.
