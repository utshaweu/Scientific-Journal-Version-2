Join as the Editorial Board Member or Reviewer

World journal of Biotechnology and Bioengineering invites you to join the WJBB editorial board as one of its distinguished members or you may join as a reviewer. If you would like to join as an editorial board member or a reviewer, please visit our website www.w-jbb.com.

Please send us your resume via e-mail: info@w-jbb.com. The Educational qualification of a PhD degree is required to be an editorial board member or a reviewer. Once selected, your information will be displayed in the WJBB website. These honorary positions are voluntary, hence no remuneration is offered.
