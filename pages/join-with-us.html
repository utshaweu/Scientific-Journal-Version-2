Join as the Editorial Board Member or Reviewer

World journal of Biotechnology and Bioengineering invites you to join the WJBB editorial board as one of its distinguished members or you may join as a reviewer. If you like to join please complete the below form and also send us your resume via e-mail: info@w-jbb.com. The Educational qualification of a PhD degree is required to be an editorial board member or a reviewer. Once selected, your information will be displayed in the WJBB website. These honorary positions are voluntary.
